# Privacy Policy – DocuNest

**Effective Date:** January 2026  

DocuNest ("the App") is a mobile application developed by **Sailesh Pulukuri** ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

DocuNest is designed to operate primarily offline and prioritizes local data storage and user privacy.

---

## Information We Collect

DocuNest does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share personal information with external servers.

The App does **not** require account registration and does not collect:

- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Contacts
- Device identifiers
- Analytics or tracking information
- Advertising data

All information entered into the App remains on the user's device.

---

## Local Data Storage

DocuNest stores user data locally on the device. This may include:

- Profile names and relationships
- Folder or bundle names
- Document titles and notes
- Images and PDF files selected by the user
- Reminder dates and settings

This data is saved using local storage and a local database. No cloud storage or remote synchronization is used.

---

## Security

DocuNest relies on the device’s built-in security features, which may include:

- Device PIN, pattern, or passcode
- Biometric authentication such as fingerprint or face unlock

Authentication information is managed by the device’s operating system. The App does not store passwords or biometric data externally.

---

## Internet Usage

DocuNest functions without requiring an internet connection.

The only external link provided within the App is this Privacy Policy page, which opens in the device’s browser. Accessing this page does not result in data collection by the App.

---

## Data Sharing

DocuNest does **not** sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Since the App operates locally on the device, no user information is transmitted externally.

---

## Children’s Privacy

DocuNest is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Data Deletion

Users have full control over their data within the App.

- Profiles, folders, and documents can be deleted at any time.
- Uninstalling the App will remove locally stored data from the device.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes or improvements to the App. Updates will be posted on this page.
